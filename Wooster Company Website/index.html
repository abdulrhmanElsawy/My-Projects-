ABOUT US

Idea Contest is an ambitious initiative from Idea Box, a subsidiary of Root Cap Business Consultants, organized with an aim to encourage, develop and support budding entrepreneurs.

he program presents an excellent platform for individuals to share their business ideas, interact with mentors, make valuable connections, receive feedback about their business plans, and compete for awards. Suitable business plans may attract funding and investors based on feasibility. We all love ideas. Not only do they keep us agile, but will also help us contribute to making our world a better place. All the great inventions and creations ideated somewhere and sometime when least expected, and grew to be the game changers as we see today. The most daunting process is to bring ideas to life. Even more baffling is to validate if they are strong enough to cultivate and invest. Ideas are born continuously. But a consistent and thriving ecosystem to validate, nurture, brainstorm, handhold and nourish these concepts to a flourishing business, and building social entrepreneurship, does not exist as much as we wish to. We are a bunch of crazy, obsessed business owners, who not only want to create the eco-system but also wish to contribute in making this country stronger through systematic engineering of these adhoc and creative ideas. Idea box provides an opportunity to throw in your idea and we will help you plough through the identification phase. A subsidiary owned by Root Cap Business Consultants, IdeaBox aims at adding more entrepreneurs to the nation. The Idea Contest initiated by The IdeaBox seeks to encourage everyone with a business idea to realize the entrepreneurial dream in them through personalized guidance from mentors.
